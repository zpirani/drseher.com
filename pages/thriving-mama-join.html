Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my membership at any time?
Yes — monthly members can cancel at any time and won't be charged again. Annual memberships are non-refundable but give you access for the full year.
Is this a substitute for medical advice?
No. The Thriving Mama community provides education, support, and wellness guidance only. It does not replace personalised medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you need medical care, please contact your GP or healthcare professional.
What happens after I join?
You'll receive a welcome email within minutes with your login details, a guide to getting started, and the date of the next live Q&A session.
Are the live sessions recorded?
Yes — all live Q&A sessions and workshops are recorded and added to the resource library, so you never miss out even if you can't attend live.
Is this suitable if I'm pregnant, not yet a mother, or have older children?
Absolutely. The community is open to anyone on the motherhood journey — from those trying to conceive, through pregnancy, postpartum, and beyond.
