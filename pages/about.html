Hi, I'm Dr. Seher.

After becoming a mother, I found myself searching not only for authentic connection but also rediscovering who I am. Going through this journey alone with a little baby was tough, and it made me realise how much support and connection matter.

With over a decade of experience in clinical practice and a deep passion for the power of coaching, I am dedicated to empowering women to take control of their health and wellness during the most transformative years of their lives.

My Background

I chose medicine because of my love for science and my personal belief in helping people. Even when others told me the journey would be too difficult, I kept going — and in the blink of an eye, I can see that a growth mindset has been crucial to everything I have achieved.

Realising how powerfully mindset influences both achievement and living a truly fulfilling life itself, I am continually fascinated by coaching, and that is what led me to become a coach.

After becoming a mother, I have seen first-hand the challenges of accompanying myself while balancing motherhood, wellness, and identity — and that is what inspired me to empower mothers to do the same.

My Mission

My mission is to help mothers move beyond survival mode by supporting them mentally, physically, and emotionally — a thriving, through evidence-based coaching and community.

"Motherhood is a journey that requires courage, patience, and community. My goal is to make sure no mama ever navigates the challenge of motherhood alone."
— Dr. Seher
